<p><p>The front dash camera market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel dash cameras focus only on the front view of the vehicle, capturing essential driving footage. They are typically more affordable and easier to install. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras provide coverage from multiple angles, usually including front, rear, and sometimes side views. These systems offer comprehensive protection and better surveillance of incidents, making them ideal for fleet monitoring and ensuring enhanced security for personal vehicles.</p></p>
