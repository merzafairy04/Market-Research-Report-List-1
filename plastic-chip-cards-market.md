<p><p>The plastic chip card market comprises primarily two types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a card reader for data transfer, commonly used in payment systems and secure access applications. On the other hand, contactless cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, allowing users to make transactions by simply tapping the card near a reader, enhancing convenience and speed. Both card types are integral to modern financial transactions, identity verification, and access control.</p></p>
