<p><strong>The Fiber Collimators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Fiber Pigtail</li><li>Multimode Fiber Pigtail</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber collimators market is categorized into single-mode fiber pigtails and multimode fiber pigtails. Single-mode fiber pigtails are designed for long-distance transmission, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which minimizes signal loss and enhances bandwidth. In contrast, multimode fiber pigtails support multiple light modes, making them ideal for short-distance applications, such as within buildings or campus networks. Both types serve essential roles in optical communication, contributing to efficiency and performance in various telecommunication systems.</p></p>
