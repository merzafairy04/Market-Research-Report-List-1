<p><p>The electric hospital beds market is categorized into semi-electric and full electric beds. Semi-electric beds allow for adjustable positioning of the head and legs through electric controls, while manual adjustments are needed for height. Full electric beds, on the other hand, offer complete control over height, head, and leg adjustments via electric motors, enhancing patient comfort and ease of caregiver access. Both types cater to different needs in healthcare settings, balancing functionality and cost considerations.</p></p>
