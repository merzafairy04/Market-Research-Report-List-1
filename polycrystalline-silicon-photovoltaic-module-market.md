<p><p>The polycrystalline silicon photovoltaic module market is divided into two main types based on design: one-sided and double-sided modules. One-sided modules convert sunlight into electricity from a single face, making them the traditional choice for solar installations. In contrast, double-sided modules harness sunlight on both sides, capturing reflected light and improving overall efficiency, especially in locations with high albedo surfaces. This dual capability allows for greater energy production, enhancing their appeal in competitive solar markets.</p></p>
