<p><strong>The Compressor Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Seals</li><li>Non-contact Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor seals market comprises two primary types: contact seals and non-contact seals. Contact seals engage directly with rotating components, providing effective sealing but experiencing wear over time. They ensure minimal leakage in high-pressure environments. In contrast, non-contact seals maintain a gap, preventing friction and wear, which leads to longer lifespans and reduced maintenance. Non-contact seals are suitable for applications requiring higher speeds but may allow more leakage. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing compressor efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
