<p><strong>The Surgical Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image intensifier C-arms</li><li>Flat Panel Detector C-arms (FPD C-arms)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical imaging systems encompass two primary market types: image intensifier C-arms and flat panel detector C-arms (FPD C-arms). Image intensifier C-arms utilize a traditional imaging technology that enhances low-level radiation to produce high-contrast images, making them suitable for a variety of surgical procedures. In contrast, FPD C-arms employ advanced digital flat panel detectors for superior image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and faster image processing. Both types play crucial roles in providing real-time imaging during surgeries to enhance accuracy and outcomes.</p></p>
