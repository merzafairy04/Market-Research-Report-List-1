<p><strong>The Automotive Night Vision Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Automotive NVS</li><li>Passive Automotive NVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Night Vision Systems (NVS) enhance visibility in low-light conditions, improving driver safety. Active Automotive NVS utilizes infrared light emitted from the vehicle to illuminate the surroundings, allowing drivers to detect pedestrians and obstacles beyond the reach of standard headlights. In contrast, Passive Automotive NVS relies on thermal imaging to detect heat signatures from objects in the environment, providing a clearer view of potential hazards without additional light sources. Both types contribute significantly to nighttime driving safety.</p></p>
