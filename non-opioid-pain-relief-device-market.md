<p><p>The non-opioid pain relief device market comprises various types designed to manage pain without opioids. Implantable devices, such as spinal cord stimulators, are surgically placed within the body to provide targeted relief. Non-implantable devices, like transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) units, offer portable pain management solutions. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat generated from radio waves to disrupt nerve function, providing long-lasting relief. Emerging technologies also include innovative methods like neuromodulation and bioelectronic therapies, expanding treatment options for chronic pain sufferers.</p></p>
