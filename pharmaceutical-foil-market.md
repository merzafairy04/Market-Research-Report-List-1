<p><p>The pharmaceutical foil market includes two primary types: cold forming aluminum foil and tropical aluminum foil. Cold forming aluminum foil is designed for packaging solid dosage forms, offering a moisture and light barrier that enhances product stability and shelf life. Tropical aluminum foil, on the other hand, is specifically formulated to withstand high humidity and temperature conditions, making it ideal for tropical climates. Both types prioritize the protection of pharmaceutical products from environmental factors, ensuring safety and efficacy.</p></p>
