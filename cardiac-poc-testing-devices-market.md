<p><p>The cardiac point-of-care (POC) testing devices market is segmented into several types, including troponin tests, brain natriuretic peptide (BNP) tests, and others. Troponin tests are crucial for diagnosing myocardial infarction by detecting cardiac-specific proteins. BNP tests assess heart failure by measuring levels of the hormone produced in response to cardiac stress. Other tests may include markers for ischemia, electrocardiogram devices, or risk assessment tools, providing comprehensive monitoring and rapid assessment of cardiac conditions at the patient's bedside.</p></p>
