<p><p>The apple juice concentrate market primarily includes two types: cloudy and de-ionized apple juice concentrates. Cloudy apple juice concentrate retains the natural pulp and fiber from apples, offering a rich flavor and a more authentic taste, often preferred in organic products. De-ionized apple juice concentrate, on the other hand, undergoes a purification process to remove minerals and impurities, resulting in a clearer, more refined product. This type is commonly used in beverages and food products requiring a consistent flavor without cloudiness.</p></p>
