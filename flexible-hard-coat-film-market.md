<p><p>The flexible hard coat film market includes three primary types: TAC (Triacetate Cellulose), PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), and PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate). TAC is known for its excellent optical clarity and is commonly used in lenses and displays. PET offers strong durability, heat resistance, and is widely utilized in packaging and electrical applications. PEN provides superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-performance electronic and optical applications. Each type serves distinct industry needs based on unique properties.</p></p>
