<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarm</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household smoke alarm market primarily consists of photoelectric smoke alarms and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles, providing quicker responses to smoldering fires. They are particularly effective in detecting slow-burning fires often associated with furniture and household items. Combination smoke alarms integrate both photoelectric and ionization technologies, offering comprehensive protection against various fire types, including fast-flaming and smoldering fires, enhancing overall safety in residential settings.</p></p>
