<p><p>The flame retardant cable market comprises three main types based on smoke and halogen content. Low-smoke halogen-free cables emit minimal smoke and contain no halogens, making them ideal for environments where safety is a top priority. Low-smoke low-halogen cables also produce reduced smoke but include some halogen content, offering a balance between performance and safety. In contrast, low-smoke halogen cables provide effective flame resistance while emitting non-toxic gases, suitable for applications where fire safety is essential.</p></p>
