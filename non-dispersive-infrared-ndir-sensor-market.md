<p><p>A Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensor is a sophisticated device used to detect and measure gases based on the principle of infrared absorption. The sensor operates by emitting infrared light through a sample gas, measuring the wavelength of light absorbed by specific gas molecules. This technology is widely utilized in applications such as environmental monitoring, industrial processes, automotive, and air quality assessment due to its accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.09% during the forecast period.
