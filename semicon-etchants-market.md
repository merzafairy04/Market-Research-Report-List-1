<p><ul><li>WetEtchingAgents</li><li>DryEtchingAgents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etchants market is divided into two primary types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve chemical solutions that selectively remove material from the semiconductor wafer's surface through immersion or spray methods. They are ideal for achieving high precision in patterning. In contrast, dry etching agents utilize gaseous mediums to etch materials through techniques like plasma etching and reactive ion etching. Each type serves specific applications in the semiconductor manufacturing process, focusing on precision and efficiency.</p></p>
