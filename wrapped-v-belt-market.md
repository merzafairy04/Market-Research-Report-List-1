<p><ul><li>Classical Wrapped V-Belt</li><li>Wedge Wrapped V-Belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wrapped V-Belt market is primarily categorized into Classical Wrapped V-Belts and Wedge Wrapped V-Belts. Classical Wrapped V-Belts are widely used for general applications, providing flexibility and effective power transmission with their trapezoidal cross-section. Wedge Wrapped V-Belts, on the other hand, offer a narrower design and higher load-carrying capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty machinery and higher horsepower requirements. Both types are essential for various industries, such as automotive, agriculture, and manufacturing, ensuring efficient performance in belt-driven systems.</p></p>
