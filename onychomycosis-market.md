<p><p>Onychomycosis, a fungal nail infection, is categorized into several types based on its presentation and location. Distal subungual onychomycosis primarily affects the nail bed and distal part of the nail. White superficial onychomycosis presents as white patches on the nail surface. Proximal subungual onychomycosis starts at the nail's base. Candida onychomycosis is caused by yeast, often affecting toenails in moist environments. The "others" category includes rare types and mixed infections, contributing to diverse treatment approaches in the market.</p></p>
