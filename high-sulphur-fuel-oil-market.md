<p><p>The High Sulphur Fuel Oil (HSFO) market includes two primary types: Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO) and Light Fuel Oil (LFO). HFO, characterized by its high viscosity and density, is typically used in maritime and industrial applications, providing cost-effective energy despite environmental concerns. LFO, being lighter and less viscous, is used in residential heating and some industrial processes, offering cleaner combustion properties.
