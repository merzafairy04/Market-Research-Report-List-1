<p><p>The Wafer Protective Film market is segmented into two main types: UV Curing and Non-UV Curing. UV Curing films are designed to harden when exposed to ultraviolet light, providing strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Non-UV Curing films, on the other hand, do not rely on UV light for curing, offering alternative methods like heat or moisture curing. Each type caters to specific needs in wafer protection, influencing their applications in various industries.</p></p>
