<p><p>The cooled SWIR camera market comprises three main types: TE Cooled, Air Cooled, and Water Cooled. TE Cooled cameras use thermoelectric technology to maintain low temperatures, enhancing sensitivity and reducing noise for high-performance imaging. Air Cooled cameras utilize ambient air for cooling, offering a simpler and more portable solution, though with moderate performance. Water Cooled cameras are designed for industrial applications, using water circulation to achieve optimal cooling, ideal for long-duration use and demanding environments. Each type caters to specific application needs.</p></p>
