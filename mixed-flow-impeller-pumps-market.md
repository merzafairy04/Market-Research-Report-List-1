<p><p>Mixed flow impeller pumps are designed to handle fluid movement by combining elements of both centrifugal and axial flow. The market for these pumps is categorized into multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps consist of several impellers arranged in series, enabling them to generate higher pressures for applications requiring significant elevation changes. Conversely, single-stage pumps have a single impeller, making them suitable for lower pressure applications, providing efficient flow for various industrial and agricultural uses.</p></p>
