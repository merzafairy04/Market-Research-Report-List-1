<p><p>Pharmaceutical grade sodium carbonate is available in two primary market types: fine and coarse. Fine sodium carbonate has a small particle size, enhancing its solubility and making it suitable for precise pharmaceutical applications, including formulations and laboratory uses. In contrast, coarse sodium carbonate has larger particles, providing bulk uses such as in the manufacturing of other chemicals or as a buffering agent. Both types adhere to stringent purity standards, ensuring safety and efficacy in pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
