<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Residential Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential ventilation systems market comprises various types tailored to enhance indoor air quality. Exhaust ventilation systems expel stale air, creating a negative pressure that draws in fresh air through leaks. Supply ventilation systems introduce fresh air while preventing contaminants from entering, promoting positive pressure. Balanced ventilation systems combine both exhaust and supply methods, maintaining neutral pressure and air quality. Energy recovery systems optimize efficiency by transferring heat and moisture between incoming and outgoing air, reducing energy costs while ensuring comfort.</p></p>
