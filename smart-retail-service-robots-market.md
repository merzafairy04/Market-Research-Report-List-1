<p><p>The Smart Retail Service Robots market can be categorized into three types based on power source: Rechargeable, Plug-in, and Swappable. Rechargeable robots use built-in batteries that recharge when not in use, offering flexibility and mobility. Plug-in robots rely on direct electrical connection for operation, providing continuous power but limiting movement. Swappable robots feature easily replaceable battery packs, enabling uninterrupted service by allowing quick battery changes, thus minimizing downtime and optimizing operational efficiency in retail environments.</p></p>
