<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>100% Solids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy concrete floor coatings market consists of three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and 100% solids. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, low in odor, and easy to clean, making them ideal for residential applications. Solvent-based coatings offer strong adhesion and durability, suitable for industrial environments but contain higher levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). 100% solids coatings provide maximum thickness and durability with no solvents, making them ideal for high-performance applications where resilience is crucial.</p></p>
